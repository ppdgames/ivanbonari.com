Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

This site doesn't have user accounts, sign-ups, or a comment system. There's no backend — it's a plain set of static pages. This page explains the few places where visitor data touches this site at all.

Fonts

All fonts used on this site (Montserrat, Inter) are hosted directly on this domain. Nothing is loaded from Google Fonts or any other third-party font service, so your browser never sends a request — or your IP address — to Google just to render this page.

Newsletter & social links

The "Subscribe" button on this site links out to Substack. If you choose to subscribe there, Substack collects your email address under its own privacy policy — this site doesn't see or store it. The same applies to any other platform linked from the footer (Amazon, Goodreads, Telegram): once you click through, you're on their site, governed by their rules.

Language preference

When you pick EN or RU, that choice is saved in your browser's local storage so the site remembers it next time. This stays on your device — it isn't sent to any server or used for tracking.

Hosting & server logs

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Like virtually any web host, GitHub's servers keep standard access logs (which include visitor IP addresses) for security and reliability — this is outside this site's control and not something built for this site specifically.

Cookies & analytics

This site doesn't set cookies and doesn't run any analytics or tracking scripts. If that ever changes, this page will be updated to reflect it.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the update will be reflected here with a new "last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent via Telegram or through the contact options listed on the About page.
