Adventurers

Adventurers Series – A Fast, Sarcastic, and Wildly Unpredictable Journey Through Worlds Unknown

What happens when reality just isn't enough?

For some, adventure is an escape. For others, it's a way of life. The Adventurers series is a chaotic, humor-packed ride through different worlds—each more dangerous, ridiculous, and strangely familiar than the last. It pokes fun at clichés, bends the rules of classic fantasy and sci-fi, and never takes itself too seriously… until it does.

Packed with sharp wit, explosive action, and a cast of heroes who'd rather joke their way through trouble than take the world at face value, this series is perfect for those who love adventure with a side of not taking things too seriously.

Adventurers: Prologue
An Unexpected Journey Into the Heart of the Game

What happens when reality hits 'pause' and you're thrown into the game?

For Arthur, aka Dhol, and his friends, a typical night after gaming takes a mind-bending twist when they're pulled into a fantasy world filled with magic, monsters, and mayhem. Their mission?

• Find a way back home before their virtual vacation becomes a permanent stay.
• Figure out the rules of this bizarre new reality.
• And most importantly—have the time of their lives while doing it!

But this adventure isn't just about quests, epic battles, or infiltrating the Fortress. Along the way, Arthur and his crew encounter in-game characters they never met before, forging unexpected friendships with these virtual companions. When a cunning thief and a shy healer decide to follow them back into the real world, Arthur and his best friend realize the adventure is far from over—it's only just begun.

Packed with laughs, action, and pop culture Easter eggs, Adventurers: Prologue is a hilarious tale of bravery, camaraderie, and the thin line between games and reality.

Will they conquer the game—or will the game conquer them? And most importantly, where does reality end and the adventure begin?

Adventurers: Act I
Into the Wastelands

Arthur and Vince thought they finally made it home. You know, back to the real world, with Wi-Fi, boring work, and no risk of being eaten by a dragon. But somehow, their adventure buddies from the last game tagged along. Oops.

To make things even better (or worse?), Arthur—our resident warrior with far too little patience—convinced the crew to try a new game. Because what could possibly go wrong? Well… everything. Now they're knee-deep in the Wastelands—a post-apocalyptic desert filled with raiders, strange towns, and a suspiciously high number of things that shoot.

Their mission? Find their missing friend Max, who got here first and somehow became a legend. Also, figure out if the fabled Center of the Wastelands—a mystical green oasis in the desert—is real. No pressure.

A fast-paced, sarcastic, and explosively fun adventure packed with pop culture references, chaotic battles, and a mystery that's definitely not as grand as everyone thinks.

Hunters

Grim tales from a world that has already ended

Lands reborn after a forgotten catastrophe. Civilizations rebuilt on the ruins of the past. And with them — they came back. Monsters. Mutants. Remnants of something that was never meant to exist.

Most are gone. People forgot. But the darkness is real — and within it, Hunters walk. Mercenaries with special abilities, soldiers bound to the state, and sometimes just ordinary people. Those who chose — or were chosen — to fight what others no longer believe in.

If Hunters exist… there must be something out there to hunt.

Everyone has their own path. But all lead to one.
